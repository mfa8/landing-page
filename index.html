Find Your Skincare Routine.

The simplest way to discover what works for you to achieve healthy skin.

What is our solution?

Struggling with skincare issues is a rite of passage for all of us, many people struggle with finding a routine that works for them and their skin, especially those who aren't knowledgeable of common skin care regimens. Our solution uses facial recognition, using the Face++ API, along with an assesment to detect possible skin health issues, then recommending products and routines to achieve healthy skin for all. Our solution essentially uses facial recognition technology and machine learning to identify and solve common skincare issues.
